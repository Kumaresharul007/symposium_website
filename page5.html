Bid Wars


Description:

Get ready to flaunt your financial finesse at our IPL Auction event. Compete with the best and turn every penny into a powerhouse player.

Format:

Round 1:
A quiz competition in round 1 to select the top 10 teams for the final round.

Round 2:
The IPL Auction is an event where students participate in groups of 3-4 where they use their investment skills depending upon the budget and ongoing inflations. In the IPL Auction, each team will get a virtual wallet with Rs. 100 Crores. The teams will participate in an auction and have to form a cricket team of a minimum of 10 players with available funds under their Franchise. The auction will be conducted with defined rules and conditions. The teams are required to plan their resources judiciously and articulate in the best interest of the team.

Rules:

  1. A team must buy 11 players in the Auction, otherwise, they will be disqualified.
  2. There should be at least 3 batsmen, 3 bowlers, 1 all-rounder and 1 wicket-keeper.
  3. If a team runs out of money they are disqualified.
  4. Players will be rated out of 100 and each player’s ratings will be fixed and won’t be changed.
  5. There will be no reauctioning of any player in any case.
  6. Jump bids are allowed.
  7. In case of a tie among the teams, the team with the most remaining budget amount will be the winner.


General Guidelines


1. 150 rupees is the registration fee per head for the entire event.

2. Only registered participants will be allowed to attend the event , on spot registrations will not be entertained.

3. It is mandatory for the participants to provide their college ID at the help desk for verification.

4. Proper dress code should be followed by the participants.

5. A participant is not restricted to participation in any number of events as long as the event timings do not clash.

6. All the events will commence at the scheduled time, participants are requested to be punctual.

7. Participants are requested to collect the event tag from the help desk present in the workshop block.

8. No refund on the registration fees will be encouraged.

9. Students should refrain from using abusive language, obscene display and maintain good decorum.

10. Smoking, consumption of alcohol or any such substance is strictly prohibited inside the college premises.

11. Rules are subject to changes at the discretion of the management or the event organisers.
