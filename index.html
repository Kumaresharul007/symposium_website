About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College is an autonomous Institution affiliated with Anna University, which was established in the year 1997. As a proud Institution with 26 years of academic excellence, it has been ranked as the third most preferred private Engineering college in Tamil Nadu. This Institution has been consistently providing high quality professionals by offering 19 UG, 11 PG courses, and Management studies along with a school of architecture and a school of nursing.




About ECE

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in the year 1997. This Department offers cutting-edge programs in Undergraduate, Post Graduate, and Ph.D. The Department of ECE has been cultivating a culture of research orientation in its students from the very beginning, thereby felicitating the development of high-caliber researchers. More than 95% of the eligible students of ECE are being placed in reputed companies every year. This department has been consistently producing globally competent Electronics and Communication Engineers since its inception.




About the Event


Revotronics is a national level technical symposium that is conducted every year by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering. This symposium serves as a platform for discussing upcoming trends, exploring the latest technological advancements, and sharing innovative research. Revotronics involves a total of 10 events with 5 in technical and 5 non-technical domains. All these events will feature guest speakers and experts who will share their knowledge and insights with the attendees. This symposium plays a crucial role in bridging the academic and industrial gap which contributes to the advancement of innovation and technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
