Guess Work?



Description:

This event focuses on creativity.It enhances teamwork,artistic skills, and imagination.This dynamic will exercise your mind while promoting social engagement!!This consists of two rounds to determine who has that freaking creativity skills!

Format:

Round 1: Conn–ex–ions:
The event starts with 3-5 players. You need to guess the meme from the clues given which would be in the form of movie referrals, hero, etc stuffs. The team which identifies first will be awarded with points. The team with maximum points will be entering the second round , followed by the team which has the next immediate maximum points.

Round 2: Pictionary
The top team has to send any of their two members to play the game. The mate should make his/her mate guess the meme given to him/her by drawing the things related to it. Strictly nothing should be written and shown,otherwise the team would be disqualified.

Rules:

  1. Online registration is must.
  2. Strictly adhere to the format of the game.
