Fun o’Pedia



Description:

An event aimed at achieving fun-based skill testing, which includes intensive thinking, general knowledge and basic mathematical and logical skills.

Format:

Level 0: Credit Points
Initially, each team is provided with 100 valuable points that can be utilised during their 3 contest levels as mentioned below.

Level 1: Quiz Trivia
This will be an electrifying quiz round which will test the knowledge of the world of web series, movies, sports and current affairs. It is a buzzer round. So the team that is quick to answer correctly will be awarded with points. And for every wrong answer points might be deducted.

Level 2: Tally It
This will be a challenging game where every team will be given two sheets of paper having various items along with their costs. They must select items with a total cost equal to a 3-digit number they're assigned, making sure not to repeat any item. The team that finishes swiftly will be awarded with points and for every mismatch in calculation points will be deducted based on the deviation from the total cost.

Level 3: Guess the Image
It will be a fun communication game between two teams. One team will receive an image and the other has to guess what that image represents. Initially, each team is given 5 questions to solve the puzzle. However, if they can't guess it within those 5 questions, they have the option to ask more questions. But, for every additional question they ask, 5 points will be deducted from their score.
